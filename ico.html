The Mission

  • Deploy capital into yield-generating strategies.

  • Hire professional fund managers.
  • Recycle gains back into the system (not to insiders).

It Is NOT:

  • ❌ A promise of guaranteed returns
  • ❌ A buyback machine
  • ❌ A liquidity provider

  • ❌ A passive income scheme

FAQ

What is Believe Trust?

A belief-governed system where value compounds through holding and borrowing, not trading.

Can I sell BLT?

Selling is possible but discouraged. No protocol liquidity. Heavy penalties apply.

How do I get liquidity?

Borrow against your BLT. Liquidity comes from belief, not exits.

Is this a Ponzi or yield scheme?

No guarantees, no forced inflows, no pooled funds.

Who is this for?

Long-term believers. Not traders.
